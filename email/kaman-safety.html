Below you will find instructions on how to redeem your Dollar Value Credits and select your award(s).

Please note changes in the password procedure.

  • Log on to http://www.itcawards.com/cgi-bin/itcawards.pl
  • When prompted:
    • Enter company name Kaman.
    • Enter password - the first 4 letters of your last name combined with the last 4 digits of your Social Security Number. Do not use spaces or dashes. If your last name consists of fewer than 4 letters, please use your entire last name. Examples: John Smith (smit1234); John Doe (doe1234).
    • Press the submit button.
  • Click on View Gifts button.
  • Select a Dollar Value Credit to start browsing through the award selections.  You may select a gift in any award amount to total your Dollar Value Credits.
  • Once you have found your award choice, click on the picture and then the box marked Select This Gift.
  • You may continue shopping or click on View Cart to be forwarded to the Cart Contents Screen where you can verify your award selection and check out.
  • To remove a selection, click on the Red X.If your selections are correct, click on the box marked Checkout. 
  • If you exceed your Dollar Value Credits, your personal credit card is needed to complete the transaction.
  • Complete your shipping information (i.e. Name, Address).
    • It is important to enter your correct email address.  Your award confirmation will be sent to you via email only.
    • Awards cannot be delivered to Post Office Boxes; please enter only your residential address.
  • After all of your information is entered click the button marked Process Order.
